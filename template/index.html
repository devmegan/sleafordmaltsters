Our Club Nights

We encourage archers of all abilities to join us on our club nights. We shoot on Monday and Friday evenings and are located on the The Old Football Field, Osbournby during the summer months and Northgate Sports Hall, Sleaford during the winter months.

On our website you will find all the information that you need to contact us, express interest in a course or join us for a shoot. There is also information regarding competitions around the region and how to enter. If you need anything that you can't find here, please contact our secretary for any further information you need.

If you are a current AGB member and wish to book in to come and join us for an evening, please message us using the Whatsapp or Messenger buttons on the left.
